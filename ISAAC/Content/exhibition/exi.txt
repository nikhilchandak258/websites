Exhibitions were held on all 3 days of ISAAC 2012. 
Projects on display consisted of Intel Xolo Phone, 
Autonomous line follower robot, Gesture controlled 
robot, and Bluetooth controlled robot, 5 DOF (degree of freedom) 
Robotic arm and many more. The projects made by the Conatus team 
of our college were also present for all to see, namely, an 8x8x8 
Led cube (It is an 8x8x8 cube made of 512 LEDs, and programmed on 
the Arduino board), a dynamo charger, 
a project showcasing hydroponics and a mouse controlled robotic arm.
