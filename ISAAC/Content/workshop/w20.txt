In this workshop, the participants will give the robot an eye and teach the robot to see the world like the way we human beings do, using a camera. For processing of the images, we use a tool called as MATLAB.
The applications that will be taught to the participants are as mentioned below:-
<br/>
Color Recognition Application<br/>
Ball following robot<br/>
Gesture Controlled Robot<br/>
Gesture controlled mouse pointer<br/>
Gesture controlled Windows Media Player/VLC Media Player.(demo)<br/>
