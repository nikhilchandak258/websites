Ethical hacking is a form of legal hacking that is done with the permission of an organization to help increase its security. 
This session discusses many of the business aspects of penetration (pen) testing.
This lesson starts with a discussion of confidentiality, integrity and availability. 
Finally, the lesson finishes up with the history of hacking and a discussion of some of the pertinent laws. So get ready to hack!!
