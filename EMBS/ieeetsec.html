About IEEE-TSEC

IEEE-TSEC is the largest student body of IEEE in Mumbai, which caters to its members towards co-curricular technical know-how within the college. It helps in inducing the latest technological advancements and imbibes a zealous participation among its members. We encourage our members to keep an open mind and instill in them a will to excel.Curiosity is one of the most important traits of genius and we at IEEE-TSEC aim at feeding our member’s curiosities by encouraging them to explore, discover and learn

IEEE also has four of its chapters active in TSEC namely Computer Society, Communication Society, Engineering in Medicine and Biology Society and Industrial Electronics Society. The events organized by this committee give immense exposure to new technological advancements and keeps students abreast of the latest technical happenings.

ACHIEVEMENTS OF IEEE-TSEC

  • Bombay Times voted ISAAC as the Largest Technical Festival under the Mumbai University. It was adjudged the second largest Technical Festival in Mumbai after IIT Bombay's technical festival.
  • Largest IEEE Student Branch in the world in the year 1998.
  • The IEEE-TSEC website www.ieee-tsec.org has repeatedly won the Best Student Chapter Website Contest in Region 10 (Asia Pacific) in 2002, 2003, 2005, 2007, 2008 and 2010.
  • Winner of IEEE Computer Society Best International Students Chapter Award for 1999, 2002 and 2003, 2006.
  • Our students have repeatedly received the Richard E. Merwin Award for their outstanding contribution towards the development of the society and branch.
  • EMBS of IEEE-TSEC won the Outstanding Performance Award in 2006.
  • Our students have being consistently winning the “UPSILON PI EPSILON” international award by IEEE Computer Society for academic excellence.
